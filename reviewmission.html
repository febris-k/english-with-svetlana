👩‍🏫 Teacher Notes
Topic: A1 revision: verb be, countries/nationalities, possessives, basic profile language, sound discrimination.
Grammar answers: Are; is; your; aren’t; you; Is.
Odd one out: France; today; thirteen; Polish; Europe; Germany; door; repeat.
Pronunciation: hi; bye; his; Poland; table; German.
Profile answers: She’s; is; Her; are; Brazilian; Their; My; ’m not; Scotland; they’re.
Watch for: confusing country/nationality, Her/His/Their, negative “I’m not”, and pronunciation by spelling not sound.
