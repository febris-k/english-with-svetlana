👩‍🏫 Teacher Notes

Level: A1. Theme: in a hotel / checking in. Skills: vocabulary, functional grammar, speaking, short dialogue reading, pronunciation of polite intonation.

Answers: lift, single, double, bar, reception, ground floor. Checking in: Can you spell your surname, please? / Can you sign here, please? / Here’s your key. Would you like…? Yes, please / No, thank you. Social English: right, am, Thank you, welcome, Can, Sure, holiday, student, think, like.

Watch for: students may say “I have reservation” without a; “on holidays” instead of “on holiday”; “Would you like coffee?” is OK, but here practise “a coffee”.
